Screen Recording Instructions
 
If the "Record Screencast" on the interface does not work, because of technical issues, please use other options to record your screen as below.

  1. In the QuickTime Player app on your Mac, choose File > New Screen Recording to open Screenshot and display the tools.
  2. Click Record.
  3. To stop recording, click the Stop Recording button in the menu bar (or use the Touch Bar).

  1. Press the Windows logo key + G on your keyboard to open Xbox Game Bar
  2. Select Record from now (the circle) to start a clip.
  3. To stop recording, press Stop Recording (the square) to end it.

  1. Press Ctrl + Alt + Shift + R on your keyboard to open screen-recording pop-up window.
  2. Click on the red Record button to start recording.
  3. To stop recording, click on the Red Button in the system tray at the top-right corner.
